üöÄActivation Function :

  • Ideal Activation Funcion: Non linear, Differentiable, Computationally inexpensive, Zero-centered or normalized, Non-Saturating like in range (0,1) or (-1,1), Occur vanishing GD
  • Sigmoid Funcation: Binary Clasification, Non linear, Differentiable, Saturated Function(Vanishing Gradient), Non Zero Center,
  • Tanh Funcation: Non linear, Differentiable, Zero Center, Saturated, Computationally Expensive
  • Relu: Non linear, Non Saturated, Computationally inexpensive, Convergence, Non Zero Center
  • Dying Relu Problem: Set Low learning Rate, Bias +, Variant of Relu, Leakly Relu(No saturated, Easily Computed, Close to 0 Center), Paramteric Relu, ELU, SELU
